Sunrise Alarm

Wake is when the screen reaches full brightness (your get-up time). Ramp before wake is how long the light brightens leading up to Wake — e.g. Wake 10:00 PM and 20m ramp means dimmest at 9:40 PM, brightest at 10:00 PM. Wake uses 12-hour AM/PM (or 24-hour like 19:35). Black until ramp starts; stays bright after Wake until Arm or Disarm.
